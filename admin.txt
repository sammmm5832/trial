# Adding admin role.

Manage daily operations – Oversee schedules, files, communications, and office tasks.

Provide administrative support – Assist staff with documentation, reports, and correspondence.

Coordinate communication – Serve as a link between departments and external contacts.

Maintain records and systems – Organize data, manage IT systems (if applicable), and ensure compliance.

Support HR and finance tasks – Help with hiring processes, employee records, budgets, and expense tracking.
